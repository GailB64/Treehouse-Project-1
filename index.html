Background

I’m an aspiring web designer with a very diverse background. I am an artist with extensive experience in ceramics, printmaking, drawing and painting. I see web design as a new artistic medium that can be used to effectively and efficiently communicate information in a creative manner.

  • HTML

  • JavaScript

I would like to work independently with clients to impliment their online goals with a visually impressive and effective web presence.
